Planet Pratero

A fictional planet by Turito

Introduction

Pratero (formerly 'KEARTH') is a fictional planet created by Turito Yuenan (me), originally for an Undertale AU called KEARTHTale. The KEARTHTale AU dated back to mid 2019, with the intention to extend Undertale's worldbuilding.

Before June 2022, KEARTHTale and planet KEARTH were separate works when they are merged and renamed to planetPratero the worldbuilding FanFiction
